Rwby Vol 2

I guess you can sense the theme here, Rwby Vol 2 follows their beloved genre of rock and their most popular song on this album was "Caffiene" which sky rocketed them further into fame.

Rwby Vol 3

Rwby Volume 3 comes out with a couple of old songs remodled or remade, they combine a couple of their old songs and put them in to one big instrumental called "R.W.B.Y vs F.N.K.I" however their most popular song on this album is "When it Falls" once again showcasing that rocky genre but also displaying new sounds that they are experimenting with, almost like they are trying to tell a story.

  • Divide
