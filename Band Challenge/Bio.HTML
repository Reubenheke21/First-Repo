TEAM RWBY

RWBY is a band of 4 girls, Weiss who is the lead singer, Yang who plays Drums Ruby who plays guitar and Blake who plays Bass. They started playing in high school called Beacon High and began moving onto bigger venues until one day they were signed by a gentleman called Qrow. Their genre they cover is mostly rock and their most popular song as voted by their fans is one of their orignal called "This will be the day"
